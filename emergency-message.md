LEAS ALERT | WINTER WEATHER AWARE

WHERE... The Government of Len has issued a WINTER WEATHER ALERT for the District of Jojo, Grizzly Province, Riley Province, and The Allied Territories of Oakley and Pibble.

WHEN... The temperature will begin dropping at arround 18:00 (6:00PM) Thursday 26 January. Temperatures are expected to remain dangerous for at least 48 hours.

WHAT... This means that below freezing, and potentially hazardous temperatures are expected to be present within the next 48 hours.  These temperatures will be dangerously low, causing serious temperature related illnesses without proper attire. Reduce outdoor time as much as possible, and avoid travel. If travel is required, prepare for below freezing temperatures and icy conditions.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION... An ACT message will be issued if neccesary.

Government of Len | Office of the Prime Minister
